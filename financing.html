Strategic Transactions | Cross-Border Deals | Non-Regulated Advisory Support

At Zyverra, we offer consultative, strategic M&A advisory services designed to help growth-stage and mid-market companies unlock long-term value through acquisitions, divestitures, and partnerships. Our advisory role is consultative in nature—we do not broker securities, raise capital from the public, or conduct regulated investment activities under Canadian securities law.

We deliver high-touch support, market intelligence, and transaction readiness insights to support corporate and private clients navigating complex transactions. All regulatory, legal, or tax elements are executed in partnership with appropriately licensed professionals.

Our M&A Advisory Areas

1. Buy-Side Advisory We support corporate acquirers, family offices, and private investors with the strategic and operational aspects of M&A execution. Our services are non-licensed and advisory, with legal or regulated functions handled by external counsel. Includes: * Market mapping, target screening, and outreach strategy * Indicative valuation and deal modeling * Commercial due diligence and coordination * Guidance on deal structuring and offer support * Integration planning and synergy assessment

2. Sell-Side Advisory For clients considering partial or full divestiture, Zyverra supports transaction planning, preparation, and investor/buyer engagement. We do not act as an investment dealer or securities intermediary. Includes: * Strategic positioning and readiness assessment * Preparation of Confidential Information Memoranda (CIM) * Outreach to strategic and financial buyers (non-placement) * Management of the competitive bid process * Closing coordination with legal, tax, and compliance experts

3. Cross-Border M&A Zyverra provides guidance for international transactions, assisting clients in navigating jurisdictional, operational, and regulatory nuances. We do not advise on foreign law or conduct filings—these are handled by licensed global advisors. Capabilities: * Identification of international investors and counterparties * Structuring insights for tax and currency efficiency * Navigation of regulatory frameworks such as FEMA, FDI, and CFIUS * Coordination with in-country legal and compliance teams

4. Joint Ventures & Strategic Alliances We advise on the formation and structuring of joint ventures and strategic alliances that promote market access, innovation, or capital efficiency—while avoiding engagement in any regulated securities or investment activity. Capabilities: * Feasibility analysis and partner screening * JV structuring and governance planning * Strategic alignment and value mapping * Introduction to legal counsel for documentation and compliance

Disclaimer

Zyverra does not provide licensed securities, investment, legal, or tax advisory services in Canada or any jurisdiction. All M&A-related services are non-regulated and consultative in nature. Clients are responsible for obtaining advice from licensed professionals for legal documentation, financial structuring, and regulatory compliance.
