About Zyverra

Expert in resolve complex issues.

Zyverra is a globally oriented advisory firm that delivers integrated, cross-border solutions across finance, strategy, and regulatory landscapes. With a strong foundation in strategic capital advisory, commercial lending services, and global mobility planning, Zyverra empowers businesses, investors, and institutions to thrive in an increasingly interconnected world.

Our services

Why Us

Our multidisciplinary approach blends deep regional expertise with a global outlook—bridging North American capital markets with opportunities across Asia, the Middle East, Europe, and emerging economies. We specialize in:

Strategic Debt & Equity Advisory
Structuring complex financial instruments, raising capital, and advising on sustainable financing aligned with business goals
Commercial Lending & Insurance Review Services
Providing in-depth review of insurance and lending documentation to safeguard both lenders and borrowers, ensuring alignment with compliance, risk, and underwriting standards.
Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A) Advisory
Supporting buy-side and sell-side transactions with valuation analysis, due diligence, and deal execution—whether domestic or international.
Global Mobility of Capital, Business, and Assets
Enabling cross-jurisdictional movement of wealth, business operations, and investments, while addressing associated tax, legal, and regulatory implications.
Words of team leader

Kaushal Shah

Co-Founder, Zyverra

Kaushal Shah is a financial services leader with 21+ years of expertise in banking, risk management, and strategic advisory across North America and India. At Zyverra, he drives innovation in underwriting, credit risk, and commercial lending solutions tailored for data-driven decision-making.

With a background in senior roles at top-tier institutions, Kaushal brings deep knowledge in loan structuring, enterprise payments, and cross-border advisory. Known for his focus on compliance, efficiency, and fintech innovation, he plays a key role in shaping Zyverra’s growth in the financial ecosystem.

He is backed by a highly competent and experienced team of domain experts and technologists, dedicated to delivering precision-driven, scalable solutions. The team’s cross-functional strengths fuel Zyverra’s ability to serve complex client needs with agility and depth.

Our Mission

At Zyverra, we pride ourselves on a client-first mindset, operating with confidentiality, transparency, and agility. Our advisory model is built on long-term relationships, not one-off transactions. Every engagement is led by experienced professionals who combine analytical rigor with strategic foresight to deliver measurable outcomes.

We serve a diverse global clientele—including high-net-worth individuals, family offices, institutional investors, financial intermediaries, and multinational corporations—offering bespoke strategies that turn complexity into opportunity.

Our strength lies in our ability to integrate capital, compliance, and commercial clarity into one seamless advisory experience
