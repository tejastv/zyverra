Commercial Loan Underwriting & Credit Worthiness Services

  • Home
  • Commercial Loan Underwriting & Credit Worthiness Services

Overview

We specialize in delivering fast, reliable, and policy-aligned underwriting and credit evaluation services for commercial lenders. Whether you’re originating real estate, equipment, or working capital loans, our underwriting support helps you minimize risk, accelerate closings, and maintain regulatory confidence.

Our Core Services

• Creditworthiness Assessment
• Business credit scoring, guarantor strength analysis, and borrower risk profiling.
• Financial & Cash Flow Analysis
• In-depth review of financials, debt service coverage, liquidity, and future repayment capacity.
• Loan Structuring Support
• Guidance on loan terms, collateral evaluation, LTV assessment, and covenant design.
• Collateral & Third-Party Validation
• Independent review of appraisals, title reports, leases, insurance, and UCC filings.
• Credit Memo & Documentation
• Preparation of detailed credit memos aligned to your internal templates and risk policy.

Who We Support

• Commercial & Regional Banks
• Credit Unions
• Private Credit Funds
• Non-Bank Financial Institutions ( NBFIs )
• SBA / Government - Backed Lenders

Disclaimer

Zyverra does not broker or originate loans on behalf of third parties and does not offer loan placement services to the public. All underwriting and credit evaluation services are provided exclusively to institutional lenders, private credit funds, or used internally in connection with Zyverra’s own capital deployment decisions. Zyverra may, at its discretion, fund select commercial loans using proprietary capital. These activities are conducted as principal and not as an agent or intermediary. Zyverra does not solicit deposits, hold client funds in trust, or provide regulated financial advisory services to borrowers.
All services and communications are intended for professional counterparties and institutional audiences only.
