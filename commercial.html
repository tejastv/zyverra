Overview

Enabling the Strategic Cross-Border Movement of Wealth, Capital, Business & Assets At Zyverra, we provide consultative, strategic advisory services to support the compliant and efficient global movement of wealth, business operations, capital, and tangible or financial assets. Our services are structured exclusively as high-level planning and consulting engagements and do not constitute legal, tax, or regulated financial advice under Canadian law.

We work with entrepreneurs, family offices, high-net-worth individuals, and corporations to develop informed cross-border strategies tailored to international growth, wealth structuring, and regulatory alignment. All services are delivered in collaboration with or subject to validation by licensed legal, tax, or financial professionals where required.

Our Global Mobility Consulting Areas:

1. Wealth Mobility & Structuring We assist clients in navigating wealth preservation and cross-border transition strategies. Our role is consultative, focused on planning frameworks, with all legal and tax execution performed by licensed professionals. Services include: * High-level guidance on cross-border wealth structuring and domiciliation * Strategic input on trust, foundation, and family office frameworks * General support on tax residency planning and succession strategies * CRS/FATCA compliance planning (non-filing) in collaboration with certified advisors

2. Business & Corporate Expansion We offer non-legal advisory on international market entry, entity structuring, and operational rollout. Legal filings and jurisdiction-specific compliance are referred to licensed service providers. Solutions provided: * Jurisdictional assessment for entity formation * Expansion strategy and cross-border structuring concepts * General advisory on regulatory frameworks, payroll considerations, and governance * Referral-based support on intercompany agreements and IP protection

3. Capital Mobility & Investment Strategy: Our capital advisory services are consultative and non-brokered. We do not raise capital, market securities, or provide investment advice regulated under Canadian securities laws. Our expertise includes: * Conceptual structuring for inbound/outbound capital deployment * Strategic planning around FDI/FEMA/CFIUS considerations * Portfolio diversification insights (non-advisory) across jurisdictions * Introduction to qualified partners for fund placement or asset allocation

4. Asset Mobility & Reallocation: Zyverra supports clients with strategic guidance for relocating or reallocating global assets, from real estate to digital and operating assets. Legal, regulatory, or transactional services are executed through licensed third parties. Capabilities include: * Advisory on international real estate structuring and repatriation frameworks * Planning support for asset-backed financing and mobility strategies * High-value asset logistics and jurisdictional considerations * Digital and blockchain-based asset movement planning

Disclaimer

Zyverra does not offer regulated legal, tax, immigration, or investment advisory services. All guidance is strictly consultative and must be validated by appropriately licensed professionals in relevant jurisdictions.
