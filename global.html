Debt | Equity | Structured Finance (Advisory Only)

At Zyverra, we offer strategic advisory services to help businesses navigate and optimize their capital structure through tailored financing strategies. We work alongside founders, CFOs, and stakeholders to identify the most appropriate forms of capital—debt, equity, or hybrid—to support growth, expansion, or recapitalization.

Debt Capital Strategy (Advisory Services)

We provide insights and recommendations on structuring debt solutions that align with your business lifecycle, from working capital to long-term expansion needs.

Typical advisory areas include

Structuring and strategy for term loans and revolving credit facilities

Evaluating options for bridge financing and growth capital

Guidance on senior vs. subordinated debt layering

Review of potential syndicated lending options

Advisory on asset-based lending opportunities and collateral optimization

Equity Capital Advisory

Zyverra supports clients in evaluating and preparing for equity raises and investor partnerships, helping you position your business for long-term strategic alignment and financial health. Our advisory scope covers: * Private placement readiness and investor targeting strategies * Designing strategic equity partnerships and co-investment models * Advisory on joint venture structures and partnership governance * Support for minority/control transactions and shareholder exits * Family office and institutional capital mapping and introductions (non-brokered)

Structured & Hybrid Capital Solutions

We advise on bespoke capital instruments that blend features of debt and equity to support businesses undergoing transitions, high-growth phases, or balance sheet optimization. We provide structuring insights on: * Mezzanine financing strategies and investor targeting * Evaluating suitability of convertible instruments and triggers * Design of preferred equity structures for non-dilutive growth * Exploring credit-enhanced structures and risk mitigation mechanisms * Frameworks for receivables securitization and monetization of cash flows

Disclaimer

Zyverra does not offer regulated legal, tax, immigration, or investment advisory services. All guidance is strictly consultative and must be validated by appropriately licensed professionals in relevant jurisdictions.
